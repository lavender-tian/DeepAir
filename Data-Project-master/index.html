About DeepAir

DeepAir is a PM2.5 Forecasting Web Application Based on Convolutional Neural Network.

Important for Human Health

Exposure to PM2.5 increases the risk of getting lung cancer and blood diseases. We take care of health of everybody!

Consider Spatial and Time Relationship

Existing methods mainly focus on predictions on small regions. We overcome this by using CNN due to locally connectivity structure and generaliability to large scale problem.

Easy Visualization

We use map and bar chart to provide straightforward and easy presentation of PM2.5 contribution in different stations.
